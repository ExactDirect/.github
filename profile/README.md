# Exact Direct Logistics Information (ED)

## 🚀 Building Industry-Leading Logistics Platforms
Exact Direct (ED) is a technology-driven logistics information provider based in Shenzhen. We specialize in creating cross-border e-commerce logistics value through advanced technology applications, algorithms, and seamless API integration.

### 🌐 Vision & Mission
- **Logistics Connectivity:** We believe logistics should be more than just a cost; it’s a value-adding process. Our "Exchange Method" connects the best-in-class applications for every logistics operation via high-performance APIs.
- **Empowering SMEs:** We vow to provide exchange platforms and process management technologies for small-to-medium logistics companies, ensuring advanced tech is accessible to everyone, not just monopolistic players.

### 🛠 Core Products & Systems
- **Zhunyun Network (Last1mile.com):** A logistics marketplace for international e-commerce, covering global last-mile delivery, overseas warehousing, and trade compliance (VAT/Customs).
- **Shouku Container (Digitalized Consolidation):** An automated export consolidation system utilizing ED’s cargo digitalization methods, RFID tracking, and advanced WMS for maximum load efficiency.
- **TMS & LaMMS:** - **China Freight TMS:** Automating freight processes and real-time POD capture via mobile apps.
    - **Global Last Mile Management (LaMMS):** Comprehensive coverage of overseas trucking, LTL, and door-to-door installation.
- **PO Management System:** Helping international buyers manage FOB terms with real-time visibility of factory inventory and shipment data.

### 🔧 Key Technical Capabilities
- **API-First Architecture:** Rapidly connecting professional processes according to specific customer requirements.
- **IoT & Digitalization:** Integrating RFID technology, automated weighing/scanning, and QC imaging systems.
- **E-commerce Specialized:** Tailored solutions for high-frequency, small-batch, and complex trade compliance scenarios.
